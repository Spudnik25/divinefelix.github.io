FAQ

What's your typical turnaround time? ▾
Logo design typically takes 5–7 business days. Full brand identity takes 2–3 weeks. Social media packs take 3–5 days. Rush delivery available.
Do you work with international clients? ▾
Yes — I work with clients worldwide. Most of my client base is in Nigeria, the UK, and North America. I accommodate different time zones.
How does payment work? ▾
I require 50% upfront before work begins, with the remaining 50% due on delivery. I accept bank transfer, Paystack, and PayPal.
How many revisions are included? ▾
Most packages include 2–3 structured revision rounds. Additional revisions beyond this are billed at a flat hourly rate.
