How I Work

My Design
Process

01

Discovery Call

We start with a free 30-minute call to understand your brand, goals, audience, and project scope. I ask the right questions so we're aligned before any pixel gets pushed.

02

Research & Strategy

I study your competitors, analyse your target market, and develop a creative brief that guides all design decisions with strategy — not just aesthetics.

03

Concept Development

I present 2–3 distinct design concepts with clear rationale. Each concept is rooted in your brand strategy, not random creativity.

04

Refinement

We select a direction and refine it through structured feedback rounds. I iterate quickly and purposefully until the design is exactly right.

05

Final Delivery

You receive all files, formats, and documentation needed to use your brand confidently — everywhere, forever.
