About Me

The Story
Behind the Work

4+
Years of Design

Hi, I'm Divine

Growing up surrounded by bold colors, patterns, and the visual richness of Nigerian culture, I developed an instinct for what makes design feel alive. Design wasn't just something I learned — it was something I absorbed.

I started freelancing 4+ years ago, working with local businesses in Owerri and gradually expanding to a worldwide client base. Today I work with startups, tech companies, small businesses, and e-commerce brands across Africa, the UK, and North America.

I'm currently studying at the Federal University of Technology, Owerri (FUTO), where my technical curiosity meets my creative drive. I believe great designers understand how things work, not just how they look.

Design Philosophy

Design is strategy with a beautiful face

✦

Purpose First

Every design decision I make is driven by a question: does this serve the brand's goal? Beautiful is a byproduct of purposeful.

◈

Bold Simplicity

I strip away the noise until only the essential remains — then I make it impossible to ignore. Simplicity is not a lack of detail, it's mastery of it.

◎

Strategic Storytelling

Every brand has a story. My job is to translate that story into visuals that resonate with the right audience at the right moment.
